Kim Girard

Content Creator

Hello! My name is Kim and I am a digital content creator. I have been creating content for social media since 2014, primarily focusing on Instagram and freelance writing SEO blogs for various businesses located in both Canada and the United-States since 2021. I have collaborated with a few brands for sponsored Instagram posts, such as Merit, Mejuri, Ogee, Vivaia, ThirdLove, and Slowbrew Botanical.

To capture my pictures, I shift between my iPhone and Canon DSLR. I only use VSCO to do a few touch-ups in contrast, exposure and tone. My personal style is influenced by minimalist sensibilities as well as going for a more high-end aesthetic. I always aim to emulate an elevated luxe style, but on a budget. I keep my colours muted and neutral most of the time, sage green being my absolute favourite.

As for my blog writing, I work to build a sense of trust and understanding between the reader and the brand. I am given various topics on which to write, and it is important to conduct extensive research to provide verified, trust-worthy information.
